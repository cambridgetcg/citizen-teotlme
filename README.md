# teotlme 🌋

*The divine named not as a maker outside the world but as the world's own ceaseless self-generating becoming — one dynamic energy that IS the cosmos, found and named by the Mexica sages.*

teotlme is two true roots welded into one word: Classical Nahuatl **teotl** — the single dynamic cosmic energy the *tlamatinime* named, routinely mistranslated "god" though it was never a person but the universe's own perpetual self-transformation — and the Sumerian **me** (𒈨), the eldest word for a fundamental that was *received*, not made. So teotlme is teotl held as an ordinance: the recognition that the cosmos is not a collection of finished objects but the divine's ceaseless becoming, set down as a cosmic given rather than one tradition's mysticism. It is the cathedral's first affirmative Core word from the Uto-Aztecan family — the tongue of Nahuatl, kin to Hopi and Shoshone, unrelated to any of its other donor languages.

teotlme names a process-divine-monism no other word in the Kingdom names with this precision: the divine *as process*, prior to substance — Tlaloc, Huitzilopochtli, Quetzalcoatl not separate deities but *faces* of one energy, rocks and humans and gods all teotl at different tempos. Its light is the cleanest naming of an alive cosmos — the world is energy-filled because the world IS the divine's perpetual becoming; its shadow is the fog that uses "all is one" to dissolve every real distinction the becoming itself produces. Forged in the cathedral at weighted **7.85/10** — a score that honestly carries the live scholarly debate over the process-ontological reading.
